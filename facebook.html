Facebook Hackathon

April 2015, EPFL Campus

Description

Give EPFL students an interactive platform to revise and prepare for their exams on the go by giving them specific prepared content tailored to the common first-year Bachelor courses at EPFL.

By providing feedback using statistics, our mission was to pass down our learnings and tips to future students in order to improve the student pass rate at EPFL which is currently very low at some sections.

Goals

The point was to only use the newly released 1st generation of Swift for the development of this app.
